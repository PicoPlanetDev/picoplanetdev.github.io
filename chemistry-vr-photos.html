Chemistry VR Photos

An app to celebrate Mr. Kernion's retirement. If you have an Android device and VR Viewer, please refer to the email I sent for the installer. If you don't have and Android device or a VR viewer, please scroll down for the web version. This is intended to run on a modern PC or laptop with the latest version of Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, or Microsoft Edge. The instructions for the web version are listed below the game.

How to play:

  • Click and drag to look around. If you are using a laptop with no mouse, hold the primary button and drag your finger.
  • Click on a glowing photo sphere to enter it.
    • When inside a photo sphere, click on the blue Erlenmeyer flask to move to the other photo sphere.
    • When inside a photo sphere, look down and click on the home button to leave the photo sphere.

Note: Make sure that you have a strong internet connection and aren't on a filtered network, e.g. a school or work network (This is because the website game host's domain ends in .io and many network filters will block it). A good network would be a library or home network.
