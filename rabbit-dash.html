Rabbit Dash

Rabbit Dash is a VR runner game where the player is a rabbit that you need to steer to eat carrots. Please try it out by downloading the latest APK from the Files section or grabbing Rabbit Dash on the Play Store! PicoPlanet currently has six levels of stunning scenery with carrots tucked in everywhere for you to find. We are working on even more levels, which will be coming soon!
