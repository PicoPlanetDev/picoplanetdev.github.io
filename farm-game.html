A currently in-development game, Crop Craze: Farming Simulator will be the first fully immersive Virtual Reality farming simulator. In Crop Craze, you can:

  • Buy seeds, plant crops, water, and harvest them
  • Sell items at the market
  • Buy animals for extra bonuses
  • Grow flowers and plants in the greenhouse
  • Explore the countryside
  • Invest in more land
  • Roast marshmallows
  • And more!
