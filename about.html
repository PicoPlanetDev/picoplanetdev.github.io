About PicoPlanet Developing

We develop games, mostly for fun! VR Playground, our first official game, was created by playing around with the GoogleVR Demo room after figuring out how to make the player move in the direction they are facing. Rabbit Dash VR came a year after, when an object-collection runner game was suggested. And Chemistry VR Photos came from the idea of taking photos of a chemistry classroom to give as a gift to a retiring chemistry teacher.
