About PicoPlanet Developing

I develop games, mostly for fun! VR Playground, my first official game, was created by playing around with the GoogleVR Demo room (circa November 2017) after figuring out how to make the player move in the direction they are facing. Rabbit Dash VR came a year after, when an object-collection runner game was suggested. And Chemistry VR Photos came from the idea of taking photos of a chemistry classroom to give as a gift to a retiring chemistry teacher. Even more games have been made since then including the Daydream-ready Trailblazer VR and a memory puzzle game, Puzzle VR.
Now, I am working on games for 6DOF VR headsets like the Oculus Rift, Quest, HTC Vive, Valve Index, Windows Mixed Reality, and more. My current project is VR Farm Game, which you can currently download for free on itch.io and SideQuest.
To look at the behind-the-scenes code for my website, click here for my GitHub repository.

Contact me at picoplanetdev@gmail.com or by submitting a form in Contact and Customer Service.

If you are looking to hire me on a project, please don't hesitate to contact me to let me know. I am open to programming, electronics, web development, etc. projects and would happily work with you to get it to perfection.
