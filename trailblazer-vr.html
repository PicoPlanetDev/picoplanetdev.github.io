Trailblazer VR

Trailblazer VR is a brand-new VR game where you swing your immersive VR controller to slash trough grass and brush to find a treasure and move on to the next level.
In Trailblazer VR, you are at the heart of a fun adventure where you, the player, in a VR headset, can swing your machete and cut down the high grasses blocking your path and your vision. Take a look at the map before you start and try not to get lost in the weeds while searching for the chest hidden in the map!
Find the hidden treasure chest, and you will move on to the next level. There are currently two fun adventure levels with more coming soon.
This game requires the following hardware:

  • A Daydream-ready smartphone
  • A Google Daydream VR Viewer and Controller
If you don't have the required devices and feel confident about your tech skills, try following the guide below to use a Google Cardboard and an extra phone as your Daydream system.

Grab the APK in Files to give the beta a try! Trailblazer VR will soon be available as a beta on the Google Play store, so stay on the lookout!
Need help using an APK file? Watch the tutorials in the Files section.

If you don't have the required devices and feel confident about your tech skills, try following the guide below to use a Google Cardboard and an extra phone as your Daydream system.
You will need two smartphones, at least one of them running Android 7.0 and the other running at least Android 4.4 with a gyroscope. You will also need a Google Cardboard VR viewer with a strap (or a makeshift one attached).

  1. Install the Daydream app to the Android 7.0+ phone (Daydream phone) by downloading the latest APK and installing it.
  2. Install the Daydream Keyboard app to the Daydream phone.
  3. Install the Google Daydream Controller Emulator app to your Android 4.4+ phone (Controller phone).
  4. Pair the two smartphones using Bluetooth.
  5. Open the Daydream app on the Daydream phone.
  6. Tap the menu button in the top left.
  7. Tap the settings button.
  8. Tap VR Settings.
  9. Tap Build Version at least 8 times. Developer options should appear.
  10. Tap Developer Options.
  11. Scroll down and tap Controller Emulator Device.
  12. Select your controller phone from the list and press OK.
  13. Tap the “Skip VR entry screens” checkbox to activate it and press OK.
  14. Tap back, back until you get to the first Daydream home screen.
  15. Tap the blue button with the viewer icon or open the Daydream app you want to play (Trailblazer, maybe!)
  16. Open the Daydream Controller app on the controller phone.
  17. Place the Daydream phone into your Google Cardboard viewer and have fun! Most of the actions are self-explanatory, but to trigger a touchpad click, double-tap the touchpad. To recnter the controller and view, hold the lower (home) button.
  18. You may now use and Daydream app just by pairing the Controller Emulator phone and running the Controller Emulator app.
