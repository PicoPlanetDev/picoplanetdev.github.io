Donate

Donate to PicoPlanet! Any donation helps PicoPlanet Developing create new apps and updates and give you the content that you love. Donations also keep our apps inexpensive or even free.

One way to donate is to become a patron of PicoPlanet on Patreon. This will give you access to alpha app releases, news, ideas, and behind-the-scenes peeks at what PicoPlanet is working on.
